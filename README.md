# 📊 Sales Performance Dashboard

## 🔍 Project Overview

This project analyzes sales data from 2014 to 2017 to uncover key business insights related to revenue, profitability, customer behavior, and regional performance.

The dashboard helps stakeholders identify profit leakage, optimize product strategy, and improve overall business performance.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze sales and profit trends over time
* Identify loss-making transactions
* Evaluate product and category performance
* Understand regional sales distribution
* Provide actionable business recommendations

---

## 📁 Dataset

* Source: Sample Superstore Dataset (Excel)
* Records: ~10,000 orders
* Features:

  * Order Date, Sales, Profit, Quantity
  * Category, Sub-category, Product Name
  * Customer Name, State

---

## 📊 Key Insights

* 📉 Significant profit loss observed in certain transactions (up to -6599)
* 💰 High sales do not always translate into high profit
* 🌍 Some regions generate strong revenue but low profitability
* 🛍️ Certain sub-categories dominate sales but have weak margins
* 📦 Average order quantity is low (~3.8 items)

---

## 📌 Dashboard Features

* KPI Cards (Sales, Profit, Orders, Profit Margin)
* Sales Trend Analysis (Year-wise)
* Category & Sub-category Performance
* Regional Sales & Profit Analysis
* Top/Bottom Performing Products

---

## 🚀 Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, Charts, Slicers)
* Data Cleaning & Transformation
* Data Visualization Techniques

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Reduce discounts on loss-making products
* Focus on high-margin categories
* Optimize logistics in low-profit regions
* Implement bundling to increase order value
